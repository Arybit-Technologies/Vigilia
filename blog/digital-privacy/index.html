Protecting Your Digital Life

Stay secure online with Vigilia’s expert privacy tips and powerful tools.

Secure Your Passwords

Posted on June 15, 2025

Strong passwords are your first line of defense against cyber threats. Here’s how to stay secure:

  • Use Complex Passwords: Create passwords with at least 12 characters, mixing letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Enable Vigilia’s Privacy Tools: Use Vigilia’s personalized privacy recommendations to generate and store secure passwords.
  • Avoid Reuse: Never use the same password across multiple accounts.
  • Update Regularly: Change passwords every 6 months or after a security alert.
📶

Safe Wi-Fi Practices

Posted on June 10, 2025

Public Wi-Fi can expose your data to hackers. Protect yourself with these tips:

  • Avoid Public Networks: Use Vigilia’s Wi-Fi security alerts to detect unsafe connections.
  • Use a VPN: Encrypt your internet traffic with a trusted VPN service.
  • Disable Auto-Connect: Turn off Wi-Fi auto-connect settings on your device.
  • Monitor Connections: Vigilia alerts you if a network is compromised.
🛡️

Guard Against Malware

Posted on June 5, 2025

Malware can compromise your device and data. Stay safe with these steps:

  • Run Regular Scans: Use Vigilia’s malicious app scanner to detect threats.
  • Avoid Suspicious Links: Don’t click on unknown links or download unverified apps.
  • Update Software: Keep your device and apps updated to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Backup Data: Store sensitive data in Vigilia’s encrypted Legal ID Vault.
