How Vigilia Works

Get started with Vigilia’s comprehensive safety, privacy, and health features in a few simple steps.

📱

Download & Install

Get Vigilia from the Google Play Store, Apple App Store, or use the web app. Offline mode ensures functionality even without internet.

⚙️

Set Up Your Profile

Add emergency contacts, health conditions, and IDs to the Legal ID Vault. Customize privacy settings and enable accessibility features like haptic feedback.

🛡️

Enable Safety Features

Activate AI-powered SOS alerts, AR navigation, live tracking, or health monitoring. Use voice or gesture-based triggers for quick access.

🔐

Secure Your Digital Life

Use cyber tools to scan for malicious apps, enable incognito mode, and store evidence securely in the Blockchain Evidence Vault.

✈️

Travel Safely

Access destination-specific safety tips, cross-border SOS, and cultural guides. Track lost devices remotely for peace of mind.

📞

Stay Connected & Safe

Receive real-time community alerts, earn a Safety Score, and connect with emergency services or mental health support instantly.
