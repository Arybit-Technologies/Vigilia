Why Partner with Vigilia?

Vigilia’s advanced features—powered by AI, AR, and blockchain—offer unparalleled safety, privacy, and health solutions for your community or organization. Collaborate with us to:

  • 🤝 NGOs: Empower vulnerable populations with AI-powered SOS alerts, offline mode, and anonymous incident reporting.
  • 🏫 Schools: Protect students with real-time tracking, geofencing, and disability-friendly features.
  • 🏢 Corporates: Enhance employee safety with group plans, IoT smart home integration, and blockchain evidence storage.
  • ✈️ Travel Agencies: Offer travelers destination-specific safety tips, AR navigation, and cross-border SOS.
  • ⌚ Wearable Tech Providers: Integrate with smartwatches for health monitoring and panic buttons.

Partner Benefits

Our partnerships are designed to maximize impact and value:

  • ⚙️ Customized Safety Solutions: Tailored features like AR navigation or mental health support for your audience.
  • 💸 Discounted Group Pricing: Affordable licensing for large groups or communities.
  • 📣 Co-Branded Marketing: Promote safety with co-branded campaigns and safety score challenges.

  • 📞 Dedicated Support: 24/7 partner support and training for seamless integration.
