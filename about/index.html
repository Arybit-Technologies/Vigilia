About Vigilia

Founded by Arybit Technologies in Nairobi, Kenya, Vigilia is a cutting-edge safety app designed to protect individuals and communities worldwide. Our mission is to empower everyone with advanced tools like AI-powered threat detection, AR navigation, blockchain evidence storage, and offline functionality, ensuring safety and privacy in any situation.

Whether you're traveling, managing health, or safeguarding your digital life, Vigilia is your trusted companion, offering inclusive features for all, including disability-friendly options and multi-language support.

Our Vision

To create a safer, more connected world where everyone has access to instant, reliable, and inclusive safety solutions powered by AI, AR, and blockchain technology.
