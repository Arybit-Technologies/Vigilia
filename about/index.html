Our Story

Built by Arybit Technologies to protect people everywhere.

At Vigilia, our mission is to empower individuals with the tools they need to stay safe in an unpredictable world. Founded by Arybit Technologies in Nairobi, Kenya, we combine cutting-edge technology with a passion for safety to deliver a comprehensive protection app.

From emergency SOS alerts to health monitoring and digital privacy, Vigilia is designed to be your trusted companion, whether you're traveling, at home, or caring for loved ones.

Our Vision

To create a safer world where everyone has access to instant, reliable protection through technology.
